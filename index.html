Moken Village -
Beach Resort Island

Best escape from city life

Welcome to our new eco-friendly village! The Moken Eco Village is located in the pristine natural surroundings of Koh Phrathong, one of only a few hidden islands still remaining. From the resort you can explore two small islands Koh Pling Lek and Koh Pling Yai. You can also relax at the resort by enjoying your own private beach and by exploring lovely snorkeling points. These islands provide a natural habitat for various kinds of exotic birds and wild animals. At our resort, you can relax at the end of the day by enjoying a stunning sunset.

We wish to provide our valued guests with a first rate experience while also protecting the environment of Koh Phrathong. So at our resort, we prize environmental sustainability. All of our bungalows use solar panels to create electricity without disturbing the environment. As a result of our commitment to environmental sustainability, our guests will be refreshed by unspoiled natural surroundings.

Our resort features eleven styled bungalows, and includes two cabin bungalows. Each room is inspired by the unique history of Phrathong Island and is designed to highlight one of our island’s natural wonders: Deer House (cabin bungalow), Morning Glory (cabin bungalow), Moken, Gloriosa, Tin Mine, Lin-Haan, Cashew, Savanna, Pa-Nun, Hornbill, and Hermit Crab.
